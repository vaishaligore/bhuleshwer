Historical Significance

Bhuleshwar has a mythological & historical significance. Originally , it was a fort which was called as 'Mangalgadh'. It is said that Devi Parvati danced for Lord Shiva and from here they went to Kailash and got married. This place is very crowded during Mahashivratri.
